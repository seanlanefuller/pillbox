# Pillbox Panic! 💊

A fast-paced pharmacy simulation game where you act as a pharmacist dispensing medications to patients based on their prescriptions.

## Description

**Pillbox Panic!** challenges your speed and accuracy. You must read the patient's prescription, identify the correct medications (generic brands), and place them in the correct slots (7 days x 3 time periods: Morning, Noon, Night) in the pillbox grid. 

Can you keep the patients healthy and avoid medical malpractice?

## Features

- **Dynamic Patient System**: Multiple patients with randomly generated names and faces.
- **Complex Prescriptions**: As the level increases, patients have more complex medication schedules.
- **Pharmacy Shelf**: A variety of generic medications like Atorvastatin, Levothyroxine, Lisinopril, Metformin, and Amlodipine.
- **Timer-Based Gameplay**: Race against the clock to fill the pillbox before time runs out.
- **Score & Level Progression**: Earn points for accuracy and speed, and progress through increasingly difficult levels.

## How to Play

1. **Read the Prescription**: Check the patient's medication list on the left panel.
2. **Identify the Drug**: Find the correct bottle on the pharmacy shelf at the bottom.
3. **Fill the Pillbox**: Drag the correct bottle to the designated Day & Time slot in the 7x3 grid.
4. **Clean up**: Right-click on a slot to remove a pill if you made a mistake.
5. **Dispense**: Click the **DISPENSE** button when you've filled all required slots.
6. **Watch the Clock**: Ensure you finish before the timer at the bottom runs out!

## Technology Stack

- **Structure**: HTML5
- **Styling**: CSS3 (Vanilla)
- **Logic**: Vanilla JavaScript
